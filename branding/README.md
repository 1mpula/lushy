# Lushy Branding Package

Welcome to the official media branding kit for **Lushy**. This directory contains all the essential assets and guidelines to maintain visual consistency across our product and marketing materials.

## Brand Values
- **Vibrant**: Energetic, alive, and full of color.
- **Premium**: High-quality, polished, and aspirational.
- **Playful**: Fun, approachable, and not too serious.
- **Trustworthy**: Clean, professional, and user-centric.

## Directory Structure
- **[/logos](./logos)**: Official logo files (SVG, PNG) and variations.
- **[/colors](./colors)**: Color palette definitions and visualization.
- **[/typography](./typography)**: Font choices and hierarchy rules.

## Quick Reference
- **Primary Color**: `#FF4081` (Lushy Pink)
- **Heading Font**: `Outfit`
- **Body Font**: `Inter`

> [!NOTE]
> Always maintain clear space around the logo and do not alter the brand colors.
