Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 10 March 2026 Version: 1.0 Jurisdiction: Republic of Botswana
Lushy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of every person who uses our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using the Lushy app or website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.
1

Who We Are

Lushy is a digital marketplace connecting clients with independent beauty and wellness professionals in Botswana. Our platform operates as a mobile application and web portal, facilitating service discovery, booking, and payment.

For the purposes of data protection, Lushy acts as the Data Controller for personal data collected through our platform.

2

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

Data Category Examples Purpose
Identity Data Full name, profile photo, Omang/ID number (professionals only) Account creation & verification
Contact Data Email address, phone number Communication & notifications
Location Data GPS coordinates (when using location features) Service discovery near you
Transaction Data Payment method type, amount, booking ID Processing payments & refunds
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, search queries App improvement & analytics
Communications Data In-app messages between clients and professionals Platform functionality & dispute resolution
Device Data Device type, OS version, app version Technical support & debugging
We do not collect or store full card numbers. Payment card data is handled exclusively by our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor (DPO Group).
3

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Lushy account
  • To process bookings and payments and send confirmations
  • To connect clients with professionals based on location and preferences
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, bookings, and support enquiries
  • To resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To send marketing communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve and develop our platform through analytics and user feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations applicable under Botswana law
4

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following lawful grounds:

  • Contract performance – to deliver the services you have booked
  • Legitimate interests – to improve our platform and prevent fraud
  • Consent – for marketing emails and location access
  • Legal obligation – to comply with tax, financial, and regulatory requirements
5

Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Service Professionals – limited profile data shared to facilitate your booking
  • Payment Processors – DPO Group receives transaction data to process payments securely
  • Cloud Infrastructure – Supabase (database) and Expo (app delivery infrastructure)
  • Analytics Providers – aggregated, anonymised data only
  • Legal Authorities – where required by law or court order
We do not sell your personal data to third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.
6

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained for the lifetime of your account plus 3 years post-closure
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations
  • Marketing preferences: Until you withdraw consent
  • Support communications: 2 years from last interaction

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access Request a copy of your personal data we hold
Right to Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Right to Erasure Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
Right to Restrict Processing Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
Right to Data Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
Right to Object Object to processing for marketing or legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Officer (see Section 11).

8

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our web portal uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions and analyse usage patterns. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features of the platform.

Our mobile application uses device-level analytics (opt-in) and push notification tokens to deliver booking alerts and service updates.

9

Data Security

Lushy implements industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on our database
  • Secure authentication with token-based sessions
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery, in line with best-practice data governance standards.

10

Children's Privacy

Lushy is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will promptly delete it.

11

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes in-app and by email at least 14 days before any changes take effect. The latest version will always be available within the Lushy app under Settings → Legal.
