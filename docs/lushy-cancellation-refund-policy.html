Cancellation & Refund Policy
Effective Date: 10 March 2026 Version: 1.0 Jurisdiction: Republic of Botswana
This Cancellation & Refund Policy governs all bookings made through the Lushy platform (mobile application and web portal). By booking a service through Lushy, you agree to the terms outlined below. We encourage you to read this policy carefully before making any booking.
1

Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all users of the Lushy platform, including clients (service seekers) and professionals (service providers). It covers bookings for beauty, wellness, and personal care services facilitated through our app.

2

Client Cancellation Policy

Cancellations by Clients are subject to the following time-based rules:

Cancellation Window Refund Entitlement
More than 24 hours before appointment Full refund to original payment method
12–24 hours before appointment 50% refund; 50% retained as cancellation fee
Less than 12 hours before appointment No refund (full cancellation fee applies)
No-show (no cancellation made) No refund; professional retains full fee
How to cancel: Cancellations must be made directly through the Lushy app via the "Bookings" section. Cancellations requested via phone, WhatsApp, or other means are not recognised under this policy.
3

Professional Cancellation Policy

We uphold high standards of reliability on the Lushy platform. If a professional cancels a confirmed booking:

  • The client will receive a full refund regardless of the cancellation time.
  • The professional may receive a reliability strike on their account.
  • Repeated cancellations may result in account suspension or removal from the platform.
  • Professionals must cancel with at least 4 hours' notice via the app to avoid penalties.
4

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at the time of booking.
  • Refunds may take 5–10 business days to reflect, depending on the card issuer or mobile money provider.
  • Lushy will send a refund confirmation notification via the app and email once processed.
  • Refunds denominated in Botswana Pula (BWP) will not be subject to currency conversion fees by Lushy.
5

Service Quality Disputes

If a client is dissatisfied with a service received, Lushy operates a dispute resolution process:

  • A dispute must be raised within 24 hours of service completion via the app's "Report Issue" feature.
  • Lushy's support team will review the dispute within 3 business days.
  • A partial or full refund may be issued at Lushy's sole discretion following the investigation.
  • Chargebacks initiated directly with a bank without first contacting Lushy may result in account suspension pending review.
Please note: Lushy acts as a marketplace facilitator. We are not directly liable for the quality of services delivered by independent professionals but we are committed to fair resolution of genuine disputes.
6

Subscription Plans (Professionals)

For professionals on paid subscription tiers:

  • Subscription fees are non-refundable once a billing period has commenced.
  • Professionals may cancel their subscription at any time; access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • Lushy reserves the right to modify subscription pricing with 30 days' written notice.
7

Exceptions & Force Majeure

In extraordinary circumstances (e.g., natural disasters, public health emergencies, government-mandated closures), Lushy may extend full refunds or fee waivers on a case-by-case basis. These decisions rest solely with Lushy management and will be communicated via official app notifications.

8

Policy Updates

Lushy reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated in-app and via email at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
