01 — The Problem

Finding a reliable service provider in Botswana is broken

People rely on word of mouth. Professionals have no digital presence. Trust is absent. Money is lost to no-shows and scams.

😤

No Discovery

Customers can't find nearby plumbers, cleaners, or beauticians easily — it's all "ask a friend."

💸

No Price Transparency

There are no standard rates. Customers get overcharged. Professionals undersell themselves.

🚫

No Accountability

No ratings, no reviews, no contracts. No-shows happen constantly with zero recourse for either party.

02 — Our Solution

Lushy — a premium marketplace for local services

A beautifully designed mobile app that lets customers discover, book, and pay for local professionals with full transparency and accountability.

🔍

Visual Discovery

Pinterest-style masonry grid lets customers browse services, view photos, read reviews, and get quotes — all in one place.

📋

Transparent Quoting

Professionals send itemised quotes. Customers accept, decline, or negotiate before any money changes hands.

📍

GPS-Powered Matching

Find professionals closest to you. Customers can navigate directly to a professional or have them come to them.

✅

OTP Job Completion

Jobs are only marked complete when the customer confirms with a one-time PIN — protecting both parties every time.
