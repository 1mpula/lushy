# Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Cancellation & Refund Policy ("Policy") applies exclusively to Beauty and Wellness Professionals ("Professionals", "You") who subscribe to the services provided by the Lushy application ("Lushy", "We", "Our"). 

### 1. Platform Overview & Business Model
Lushy operates strictly as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and directory platform for Professionals. We provide digital storefronts, marketing visibility, and booking management tools. 

**Lushy is not a marketplace.** We do not process, facilitate, or hold payments for the actual beauty or wellness services rendered between you and your clients. Our payment processing is limited strictly to your recurring subscription fees for utilizing the Lushy platform.

### 2. Subscription Terms
Your Lushy platform subscription is billed in advance on a recurring basis (e.g., monthly). All subscription registrations and billing management take place exclusively on our secure website (lushyapp.com). By subscribing to Lushy, you authorize us to automatically bill the payment method on file on your designated billing date until the subscription is canceled.

### 3. Cancellation Policy
We believe in giving you full control over your business management tools. You may cancel your Lushy subscription at any time.

* **How to Cancel:** Subscription cancellations must be processed exclusively through the Lushy website. To cancel, please log in to your Professional account at **www.lushyapp.com**, navigate to your **Account Settings > Subscription**, and select **Cancel Subscription**. *(Note: Subscriptions cannot be canceled from within the Lushy mobile app).*
* **Access After Cancellation:** If you choose to cancel your subscription before the end of your current paid billing cycle, your cancellation will take effect at the end of that cycle. You will retain full access to your Lushy Professional app and its tools until the current paid period expires.
* **Auto-Renewal Notice:** To prevent being charged for the upcoming billing cycle, you must cancel your subscription on the website at least 24 hours prior to your next scheduled auto-renewal payment date.

### 4. Refund Policy
Because Lushy provides immediate access to digital profile visibility, premium booking software, and platform infrastructure upon subscription, we adhere to a strict refund policy.

* **Non-Refundable Subscriptions:** All subscription charges are non-refundable. We do not offer prorated refunds if you decide to cancel your subscription or stop using the platform halfway through your current billing cycle.
* **Exceptions:** Refunds are granted rarely and only under the following exceptional circumstances, evaluated at the sole discretion of Lushy management:
    * You were charged multiple times simultaneously due to a technical error on our gateway.
    * You successfully canceled your subscription on the website according to our policy (at least 24 hours prior to renewal), but your card was still incorrectly charged.
* **Requesting an Exception:** If you believe you qualify for a refund exception, you must contact our support team at support@lushyapp.com within seven (7) days of the disputed charge. 

### 5. Client & Professional Transactions (Disclaimer)
Lushy does not process, intermediate, or guarantee any payments passing from your clients to you. 

* **Independent Transactions:** Any service fees, deposits, or client no-show fees owed to you by a client must be handled entirely off-platform (e.g., via cash, direct bank transfer (EFT), or your independent card machines).
* **No Liability for Client Refunds:** Lushy is not liable for, and will not issue refunds, compensate, or mediate payment disputes regarding the transactions between Professionals and their clients. Your independent business's cancellation and refund policy applies directly to your clients.

### 6. Changes to this Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation & Refund Policy at any time. We will notify active Professionals of any significant changes via email prior to the updated policy taking effect.
